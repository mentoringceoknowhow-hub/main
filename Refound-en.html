Refund Policy

Discover a career in IT without needing to know how to code!

1. General Policy

We strive to provide high-quality mentorship services. If you're not satisfied with a session, you may be eligible for a refund under the conditions below.


2. Refund Eligibility

Refunds are only allowed for sessions canceled at least 24 hours in advance or in cases where the mentor is absent from the scheduled session.


3. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be issued for missed sessions by the mentee without prior notice, or for dissatisfaction related to lack of personal progress.


4. Requesting a Refund

To request a refund, contact our support team within 7 days of the session date, providing details of the issue and proof of payment.


5. Processing Time

Refund requests will be reviewed within 5 business days. Approved refunds will be processed within 10 business days using the same payment method.


6. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this refund policy at any time. Changes will be published on our website and take immediate effect.
